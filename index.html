About Me

Parker Cammans is future coder who is excited to embark on his new journey. Although Parker does not have much direct experience with coding, he has been given several chances to prove he is capable. Parker spent several years as a professional mountain and road bike mechanic. This is where parker developed a deep love for problem solving and developed a keen eye for fine details. Parker’s passions then lead him to further opportunities when he was able to, work for ENVE composites as a test lab technician. Working with such unique machines sparked Parker’s creativity which lead him to MIT’s professional Certificate in coding.
